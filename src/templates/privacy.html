Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Short Version

We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. We use it to make RepoLM work and improve it.

What We Collect

  • Account info: Email address, display name (from GitHub/Google OAuth)
  • Usage data: Repositories you analyze, content you generate, features you use
  • Analytics: Page views, session duration, device type (anonymized)
  • Payment info: Handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card number

How We Use It

  • To provide and improve the RepoLM service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you important account notifications (you can opt out of non-essential emails)
  • To show trending repositories and community content (aggregated, anonymized)
  • To debug issues and prevent abuse

Third Parties

We share data with these services as needed to operate:

  • Google Gemini: Repository content is sent to Gemini's API for analysis. This is ephemeral — we don't store prompts on Google's side beyond their standard processing.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. Stripe handles all card data under their own privacy policy.
  • Carbon Ads: We show ethical developer-focused ads. Carbon may use cookies for ad targeting. You can opt out via their privacy settings.
  • GitHub API: We access public repository data through GitHub's API to ingest code for analysis.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (session tokens). Carbon Ads may set additional cookies for ad relevance. We don't use tracking cookies beyond what's needed for the service and ads.

Data Retention

  • Account data: Kept while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Generated content: Cached for performance, purged periodically
  • Usage analytics: Retained for up to 12 months in anonymized form
  • Payment records: Retained as required by tax law (typically 7 years)

Your Rights (GDPR & Friends)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — see what we have about you
  • Export your data — download your generated content
  • Delete your data — request full account deletion
  • Correct your data — update inaccurate information
  • Object to processing — opt out of non-essential data use

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@repolm.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Security

We use HTTPS everywhere, encrypt sensitive data at rest, and follow security best practices. That said, no system is 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you promptly.

Children

RepoLM is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We'll update this policy as needed and notify you of significant changes. The "last updated" date at the top tells you when it was last revised.

Contact
